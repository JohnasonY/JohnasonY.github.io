About This Page

This page lists 3 games that were awarded the winner of Game of the Year in the recent 3 years. Game of the Year is a famous game award presented annually by the Game Awards. It is given to a video game judged to deliver the best experience across creative and technical fields. It is definitely worth trying out these games if you are interested in the genre of that game.
